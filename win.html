This M1 Garand rifle was made by Winchester Armory under the government contract for the WWII US army.

Winchester made M1 Garands Only During WWII Period and hence their Garands are Rarer and More Collectible than other manufacturers.

This M1 Garand rifle was made in May, 1945, WWII.

What makes this Garand Uber Rare is that it is both a WIN-13 Garand and an OverRuanning Series.

WIN-13 Garand were built by Winchesters using receivers that had been manufactured in 1943 and laid aside until 1945 and 13 is the lucky number of Winchester. There are only less than 40000 WIN-13 M1 Garand.

OverRuanning Series means that this Winchester Garand has a serial number falling in the duplicated Serial Number Range between Springfield Armory (SA) and Winchester (WRA) . There is only 30000 WRA overrunning of SA serial numbers. Highly Collectible!.
